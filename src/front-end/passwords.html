Password Holder

A common place for every roommate to access a shared password.

Save your password information here so that you can show your friends easily if you need to! You won't have to forget your password anymore!

Usage:

a) Press "Add New Password" and enter Account, Username, and Password information to save your password info!

b) After the password card is made, a bar with your specified account name will appear. Click on the Black card to view information.

c) Press "Edit" to modify an account's information.

d) Press "Delete" to delete an account.
